About Aple

Housing first. Dignity always. We create safe, supportive homes across Houston for people rebuilding their lives.

What We Do

Aple Property Manager helps disenfranchised individuals secure safe, stable, and affordable housing. We focus on people who are too often turned away—parole-eligible individuals, people in recovery, and SSI recipients—so they can restart with a real address, real support, and real dignity.

Our homes are quiet, well-kept, and community-minded. We coordinate with partners to layer in employment assistance, counseling, and other services that turn housing into long-term stability.

Mission

Alleviate desperation by addressing homelessness at the root—providing stable housing and wraparound support so people can overcome addiction, stigma, and disenfranchisement.

Vision

A stronger, safer Houston where supportive housing is accessible, neighbors feel secure, and every resident has a fair path to reintegration and self-sufficiency.

Core Values

  • Compassion—respect and dignity in every interaction.
  • Integrity—transparency, accountability, reliability.
  • Community—partnerships with agencies, nonprofits, and landlords.

Housing that feels like home

Calm, clean homes with standards that respect neighbors and give residents a foundation to grow.

Origin Story

Aple began after years of watching people denied housing due to records or recovery histories. Without a safe address, parole is delayed and progress stalls. We launched Aple to flip the script—secure housing first, then stack services to sustain success and reduce recidivism.

Team & Partnerships

Led by the founder, we collaborate with parole officers, trusted contractors, and social service groups (e.g., Goodwill, Salvation Army, Endeavor). Together we match residents with the right home, keep properties in top shape, and coordinate the support that keeps people moving forward.

The Problem

People ready to reenter society are blocked by housing denials. Some remain incarcerated; others land on the street—without stability, everything else gets harder.

Our Solution

Quiet, well-maintained homes plus dependable property management and coordinated services. Residents get a safe base; neighbors get peace of mind.

Why Aple

10+ years working with this community, a strong referral network, and proven operations across Houston allow us to meet demand quickly and responsibly.

Who We Serve

Disenfranchised individuals including those in recovery, SSI recipients, and people awaiting parole who need a stable address to qualify. We specialize in residents who face systemic barriers to traditional leases.
