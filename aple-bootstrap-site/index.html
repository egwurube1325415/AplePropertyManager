About Aple

Mission: Alleviate desperation by addressing homelessness and providing stable housing that empowers people to overcome addiction and disenfranchisement.

Vision: A stronger, safer Houston where everyone can access resources to thrive and reintegrate with dignity.

  • Compassion — respect and dignity for every client.
  • Integrity — transparency and accountability.
  • Community — partnering with agencies and neighbors to build safer neighborhoods.

Aple began after years working with parolees who were denied housing due to records or recovery histories. Without a safe address, reintegration stalls and recidivism rises. We founded Aple to flip that script—housing first, then employment, education, and long-term stability.

Led by the founder, supported by a network of parole officers, trusted contractors, and social service organizations (Endeavor, Goodwill, Salvation Army) to align housing with support services and jobs.
