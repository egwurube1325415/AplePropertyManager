About APM

Housing first. Dignity always. Stable, supportive homes for people rebuilding their lives in Houston.

Who We Are

Aple Property Manager provides quiet, well-kept homes for people who are commonly turned away from traditional rentals—justice-involved residents preparing for parole, people in recovery, and SSI recipients. We partner with case managers, nonprofits, and agencies to layer in employment, counseling, and benefits assistance so housing becomes long-term stability.

What We Do

  • Place residents into safe, affordable homes that respect neighbors and HOA norms.
  • Coordinate with parole, courts, VA, and providers for documentation and reporting.
  • Maintain properties with clear standards: cleanliness, safety, and quiet hours.
  • Connect residents to jobs, recovery supports, and benefits to sustain success.

Why It Matters

Without a stable address, parole can be delayed and people remain in custody—or return to the street. APM flips the script: secure housing first, then stack services that reduce recidivism, improve health, and strengthen neighborhoods.
